California Grand Jurors' Association
Santa Cruz County Chapter

To promote, preserve, and support the civil grand jury system through training, education, and outreach

About us
We are a group of former civil grand jurors from Santa Cruz County interested in continuing our service to the community.
History

The California Grand Jurors Association is an association of former and current Grand Jurors established as a non-profit public benefit corporation pursuant to IRS 501(c)(3) on February 23, 1998. The original corporation was formed on June 14, 1983 and registered as a (501)(c)(4) corporation.

The Santa Cruz County Chapter of the California Grand Jurors Association is a group of past and present members of the Santa Cruz County Civil Grand Jury. It was incorporated in 2013.
